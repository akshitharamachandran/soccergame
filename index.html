Rules

The guidelines to follow if you're trying to beat us.

  1. Place your mouse on the playing field
  2. Use the arrow keys to move your player
  3. Move the ball using your player
  4. Try to get the ball in the goal on the opposite side of the field
  5. Go! Fight! Win!
